Welcome to the best pasta recipe of all time!

Description

This pasta will be better than anything you've ever eaten. It is genuinely so good and so easy to make, and you'll enjoy so much.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Vegetables
  • Seasonings and herbs
  • Broth
  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients in separate bowls.
  2. Heat your oil in a saucepan and sear your vegetables.
  3. Stir in your broth and tomatoes, and reduce the heat to a simmer for about 20 minutes.
  4. Add your pasta in, and cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
