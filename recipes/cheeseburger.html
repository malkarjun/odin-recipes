Welcome to the best cheeseburger recipe of all time!

Description

This recipe is just a quick 20 minutes, and is super easy! All you need is a grill, a patty, and some standard burger toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat your outdoor grill for high heat and oil the grate.
  2. Whisk your egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Add the ground beef and bread crumbs to the same bowl, and mix it all together with your hands or fork.
  4. Form into 3/4 inch thick burger patties.
  5. Grill your patties for about 6-8 minutes per side, while covered.
  6. Top, serve, and enjoy!
